## Smart hydration for real life

HydroTrack calculates a personalized daily hydration goal based on your weight, activity, altitude, and local climate — and recalculates it automatically as your weight changes over time.

If you're on Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, or Zepbound, that matters more than you might think. Your hydration needs change as you lose weight. Most apps set a goal once and forget it. HydroTrack doesn't.
