## Data We Collect and How It Is Used

### Health & Body Data

HydroTrack requests read/write access to Apple HealthKit for the following data types:

- **Dietary Water** (read and write) — to log your hydration and read prior intake
- **Body Mass** (read) — to personalize your daily hydration goal
- **Date of Birth and Biological Sex** (read) — to adjust your goal for metabolic differences

This data is stored exclusively in HealthKit on your device and in HydroTrack's local SwiftData database. It is never transmitted to any server, never shared with any third party, and never leaves your device.

### Location Data

HydroTrack requests your approximate location to retrieve local weather conditions via Apple WeatherKit. Location is used in real time to fetch temperature and humidity data that adjusts your hydration goal. Your location is not stored, logged, or transmitted outside of the WeatherKit API call handled by Apple's servers. See [Apple's privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) for details on how Apple handles that request.

### Subscription Data

HydroTrack offers in-app purchases (monthly subscription, annual subscription, and lifetime access) processed entirely through Apple's StoreKit and the App Store. We do not see, store, or process your payment information. Purchase records are managed by Apple.

### Analytics

HydroTrack uses [TelemetryDeck](https://telemetrydeck.com) for privacy-preserving, anonymous usage analytics. TelemetryDeck collects no personally identifiable information and no data that can be used to identify you across apps or services. Signals are double-hashed before transmission. You can review [TelemetryDeck's privacy policy](https://telemetrydeck.com/privacy/) for full details.

---

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- No name, email address, or account required
- No advertising identifiers (IDFA)
- No third-party analytics (no Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or similar)
- No crash reporting services
- No data sold or shared with advertisers or data brokers

---

## Data Retention and Deletion

All HydroTrack data is stored locally using SwiftData on your device. Deleting the app removes all HydroTrack data from your device. Data written to Apple HealthKit remains in HealthKit until you delete it manually from the Health app.

## Children

HydroTrack is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.
